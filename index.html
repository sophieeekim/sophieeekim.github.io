Hi, I'm a Interaction Designer and Prototyper based in Pittsburgh, PA. Currently I'm pursuing a Master Degree of Entertainment Technology at Carnegie Mellon ETC. I formerly did a summer internship at HP inc. on 2018.

I'm a Designer

I’m passionate about creating highly aesthetic, user-centered design solutions that exhibit clarity and simplicity. I believe form absolutely follows function and design — regardless of medium — is something to experience and increases the value of anything it touches.

I'm a Developer

I love getting my hands dirty with complex problems that can't be Googled. I iterate my design options until the proper solution emerges. From sketching on paper to coding high-fidelity prototype, I use various tools to evaluate the design decisions and to make the solution work.
